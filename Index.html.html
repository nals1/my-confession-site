How it works
  • Senders must sign in — only they can create/edit/delete their messages.
  • Recipients can view a message without signing in via the private link.
  • Encrypted messages store only ciphertext on the server; decryption key is in the fragment (#) of the URL and never sent to Firebase.
  • Messages can't be listed publicly — tokens are unguessable.
